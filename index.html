Simple Counter

The simplest and most beautiful tally counter app. Downright.

Keep score by counting right in the list.
Full screen mode for no-look counting.
Set the count manually, add a title, and more.

Features

  • Unlimited counters. And they are shown in a beautiful, adaptive layout.
  • Count many at once. And do it right in the list, without hassle. Great for game scoring!
  • Full screen mode. For when you don't want to or can't look at the screen while counting. Just pinch to zoom in on a counter in the list, and—voilà!—the whole screen's the button!
  • Set the count manually. Get a head start! Just tap on the count while showing the options, and enter a new amount.
  • Quickly add a title. But you certainly don't have to.
  • Nice sound effects. Or, you can have the app speak the current count to you.
  • And more: Optional vibration on count, bulk deletion, option to prevent the screen from auto locking, and (of course) an awesome experience.
